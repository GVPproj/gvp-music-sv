**Graham Van Pelt** has been releasing music and touring internationally for over two decades.

<br />

His solo work spans many genres from guitar- and synth-pop to vocal house, techno and ambient music. Either as a solo act or with bands such as [Think About Life](https://www.discogs.com/artist/505942-Think-About-Life), [Miracle Fortress](https://www.discogs.com/artist/853449-Miracle-Fortress), and [Diamond Rings](https://www.discogs.com/artist/1068815-Diamond-Rings), he has performed all over the world, appeared on the Tonight Show and the Late Show, performed at festivals in Iceland, Europe, Canada, the UK and the US, and released with labels such as Arbutus Records [CA], Rough Trade [UK], Secret City [CA], Every Conversation [JP].

<br/>

He also composes for [film](/film), works as a [DJ](https://www.mixcloud.com/grahamvanpelt/stream/), and from 2021 - 2023 hosted the weekly online [radio show](/radio) "Island City" from his home on Salt Spring Island.

<br />

Since 2020 he has release four ambient records, either on his own or via Arbutus records, and has a fifth in the works for autumn of 2026.
